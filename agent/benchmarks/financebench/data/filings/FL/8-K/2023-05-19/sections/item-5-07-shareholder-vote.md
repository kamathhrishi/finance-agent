# Item 5.07.
## Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

*At the Annual Meeting, shareholders voted on the five proposals set forth below\. For more information on the proposals, please see the 2023 Proxy Statement, the relevant portions of which are incorporated herein by reference\. As of March 20, 2023, the Company’s record date for the Annual Meeting, there were a total of 93,429,371 shares of common stock, $0\.01 par value per share \(“Common Stock”\), outstanding and entitled to vote at the Annual Meeting\. At the Annual Meeting, 72,697,651 shares of Common Stock were represented in person or by proxy and, therefore, a quorum was present\.
