## Departure
                                         of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers;

Foot
Locker, Inc\. \(the “Company”\) issued a press release on November 30, 2020 announcing the planned retirement of Lauren
B\. Peters, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer\. On November 30, 2020, Ms\. Peters provided notice announcing her
intention to retire from the Company\. Ms\. Peters will continue to serve as Chief Financial Officer until her successor
assumes the role, and she will remain an employee of the Company until her expected retirement in April 2021, at which time her
employment agreement will terminate\. A copy of the press release is furnished as Exhibit 99\.1, which, in its entirety, is incorporated
herein by reference\.
