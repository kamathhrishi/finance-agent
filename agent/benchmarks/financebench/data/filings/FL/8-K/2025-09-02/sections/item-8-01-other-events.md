# Item 8.01. Other Events.
On September 2, 2025, DICK’S
Sporting Goods, Inc\. \(“DICK’S Sporting Goods”\) and Foot Locker, Inc\. \(“Foot Locker”\) issued a joint press
release \(the “Joint Press Release”\) announcing the preliminary results of the elections made by Foot Locker shareholders of
record regarding the form of consideration they wish to receive in exchange for their shares of Foot Locker common stock in connection
with the previously announced acquisition of Foot Locker by DICK’S Sporting Goods\.
A copy of the Joint Press Release
is furnished as Exhibit 99\.1 and incorporated by reference herein\.
