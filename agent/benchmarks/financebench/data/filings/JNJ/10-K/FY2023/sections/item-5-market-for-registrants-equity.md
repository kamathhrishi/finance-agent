### Issuer Purchases of Equity Securities

*On September 14, 2022, the Company announced that its Board of Directors approved a share repurchase program, authorizing the Company to purchase up to $5\.0 billion of the Company's Common Stock\. Share repurchases may be made at management’s discretion from time to time on the open market or through privately negotiated transactions\.  The repurchase program has no time limit and may be suspended for periods or discontinued at any time\. The following table provides information with respect to common stock purchases by the Company during the fiscal fourth quarter of 2022\. Common stock purchases on the open market are made as part of a systematic plan to meet the needs of the Company’s compensation programs\.
