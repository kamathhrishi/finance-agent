*At the end of 2019, our warehouses contained approximately 113\.9 million square feet of operating floor space: 79\.9 million in the U\.S\.; 14\.0 million in Canada; and 20\.0 million in Other International\. We operate 24 depots, with approximately 11\.0 million square feet, for the distribution of most merchandise shipments to the warehouses\. Additionally, we operate various fulfillment, processing, packaging, manufacturing and other facilities to support our business, which includes the production of certain private\-label items\. Our executive offices are located in Issaquah, Washington, and we maintain 19 regional offices in the U\.S\., Canada and Other International locations\.*
