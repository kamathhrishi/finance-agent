*Corning operates 108 manufacturing plants and processing facilities in 15 countries, of which approximately 35% are in the U\.S\.  We own 64% of our executive and corporate buildings of which 80% are located in and around Corning, New York\.  The Company also owns over 68% of our sales and administrative office square footage, 86% of our research and development square footage, 72% of our manufacturing square footage, and over 10% of our warehousing square footage\. For the years ended 2018, 2017 and 2016, we invested a total of $5\.2 billion, primarily in facilities outside of the United States\.
