## Item 5. Market for Registrant's Common Equity, Related Stockholder Matters and Issuer Purchases of Equity Securities.
### Market Information and Dividends
Our common stock is traded on the New York Stock Exchange \("NYSE"\) under the ticker symbol BBY\. In fiscal 2004, our Board initiated the payment of a regular quarterly cash dividend with respect to shares of our common stock\. A quarterly cash dividend has been paid in each subsequent quarter\. On February 27, 2020, we announced an increase in our regular quarterly dividend from $0\.50 per share to $0\.55 per share\. Future dividend payments will depend on our earnings, capital requirements, financial condition and other factors considered relevant by our Board\.
### Holders
As of March 18, 2020, there were 2,167 holders of record of our common stock\.
### Purchases of Equity Securities by the Issuer and Affiliated Purchasers

*On February 23, 2019, our Board authorized a $3\.0 billion share repurchase program\. There is no expiration date governing the period over which we can repurchase shares under the February 2019 authorization\.
