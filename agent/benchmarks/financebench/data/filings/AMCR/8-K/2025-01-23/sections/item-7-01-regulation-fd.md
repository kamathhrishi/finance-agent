# Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure
On
January 23, 2025, Amcor plc, a Jersey public company \(“Amcor”\), and Berry Global Group, Inc\., a Delaware corporation
\(“Berry”\), issued a joint press release announcing the filing of the definitive joint proxy statement of Amcor and Berry \(the
“Joint Proxy Statement/Prospectus”\) with the Securities and Exchange Commission\. The press release
also announced that the Amcor and Berry shareholder meetings will both take place on February 25, 2025\. A copy of the press release
is attached as Exhibit 99\.1 hereto and incorporated by reference herein\.
The
information contained in this Item 7\.01, including Exhibit 99\.1 hereto, shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18
of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended \(the “Exchange Act”\), or otherwise subject to the
liabilities of that section, nor shall it be incorporated by reference into any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended \(the
“Securities Act”\), or the Exchange Act, except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing\.
