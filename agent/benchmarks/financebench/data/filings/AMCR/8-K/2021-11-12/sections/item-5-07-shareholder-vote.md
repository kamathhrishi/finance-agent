## Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

Meeting”\) was held on November 10, 2021\. As of the record date for the Annual Meeting, there were 1,535,040,785 ordinary shares
entitled to vote, of which the holders of 1,073,536,212 shares were represented in person or by proxy at the Annual Meeting\. The results
of the items voted on at the Annual Meeting are set forth below: 1\.The
