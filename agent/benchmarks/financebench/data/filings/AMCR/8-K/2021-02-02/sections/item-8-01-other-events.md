# Item 8.01 Other Events
On February 2, 2021, the Board of the Directors of the Company approved a $200 million repurchase of the Company’s ordinary shares and of the Company’s CDIs over the next twelve months\.  Pursuant to this program, purchases of the Company’s ordinary shares and CDIs will be made subject to market conditions and at prevailing market prices, through open market purchases\.  The Company expects to complete the share buyback over the next twelve months; however, the timing, volume and nature of repurchases may be amended, suspended or discontinued at any time\.
