## Amcor plc
1\.The name of the Company is Amcor plc\.
2\.The Company is a public company limited by shares\.
3\.The Company is a par value company\.
4\.The Company has unrestricted corporate capacity\.
5\.The liability of each member arising from his or her holding of a share is limited to the amount \(if any\)
unpaid on it\.
6\.The share capital of the Company is US$100,000,000 divided into 1,800,000,000 ordinary shares of US$0\.05
each and 200,000,000 preferred shares of US$0\.05 each\.
