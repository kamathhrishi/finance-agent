# Item 8.01.
## Other Events.
As
previously disclosed in a Form 8\-K filed on March 17, 2020, the Company temporarily closed all of its stores across all of its
brands in North America, EMEA, and Malaysia from March 17, 2020 through March 31, 2020\. Further, in our Form 10\-K filed on March
27, 2020, the Company disclosed that it temporarily closed all of its stores in New Zealand\. Subsequently, on March 30, 2020,
the Company temporarily closed all of its stores in Australia\. All of these stores will remain closed temporarily, subject to
national, state, and local guidance related to the evolving COVID\-19 pandemic\. Customers can continue to shop online through our
websites and mobile apps across all of the Company’s brands and regions, and in store locations in the Asia Pacific region
that remain open\.
