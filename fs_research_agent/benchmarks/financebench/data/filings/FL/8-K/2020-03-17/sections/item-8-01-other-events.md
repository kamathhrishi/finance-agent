# Item 8.01.         Other
Events.
On March 17, 2020,
the Company announced the temporary closure of all stores across all of its brands in North America, EMEA, and Malaysia from March
17, 2020 through March 31, 2020\. The rest of the Company”s locations in the Asia Pacific region, which include Hong Kong,
Singapore, Australia, and New Zealand, will remain open subject to direction from local and national governments\. Customers can
continue to shop online through our websites and mobile apps across all of the Company”s brands and regions\.
