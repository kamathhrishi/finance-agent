## Item 2.  Properties

*Corning operates 116 manufacturing plants and processing facilities in 15 countries, of which approximately 34% are in the U\.S\.  We own 65% of our executive and corporate buildings of which 80% are located in and around Corning, New York\.  The Company also owns over 68% of our sales and administrative office square footage, 83% of our research and development square footage, 68% of our manufacturing square footage, and over 10% of our warehousing square footage\. For the years ended 2019 and 2018, we invested a total of $4\.2 billion, primarily in facilities outside of the United States\. Manufacturing, sales and administrative, and research and development facilities have an aggregate floor space of approximately 46\.1 million square feet\.
