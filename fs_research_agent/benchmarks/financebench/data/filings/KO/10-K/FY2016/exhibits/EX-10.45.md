We are delighted to confirm your new position as Chief Information Officer, Job Grade 20, with an effective date of October 19, 2016\.  You will report to me\.  The information contained in this letter provides details of your new position\.
<sub>•Your principal place of assignment will be Atlanta, Georgia\.</sub>
<sub>•Your annual base salary for your new position will be $460,000</sub>
<sub>•You will continue to be eligible to participate in the annual Performance Incentive Plan\.  The target annual incentive for a Job Grade 20 is 85% of annual base salary\.  The actual amount of an incentive award may vary and is based on individual performance and the financial performance of the Company\. <sub>•You will continue to be eligible to participate in The Coca\-Cola Company’s Long\-Term Incentive program\.  Awards are made at the discretion of the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors based upon recommendations by Senior Management\. As a discretionary program, the award timing, frequency, size and mix of award vehicles are variable\.</sub>
<sub>•You are expected to continue to acquire and maintain share ownership pursuant to the Company’s share ownership guidelines at a level equal to two times your base salary\.
