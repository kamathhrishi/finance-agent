### Security Trading Plans of Directors and Executive Officers
On December 24, 2025, Chris Nicholas, Executive Vice President, President and Chief Executive Officer, Walmart International, entered into a stock trading plan designed to comply with Rule 10b5\-1 under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934\.
