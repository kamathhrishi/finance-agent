# Item 7.01. Regulation FD.
On October 9, 2025, in a press
release dated that same date and included as Exhibit 99\.1 to this Current Report on Form 8\-K, the Company reaffirmed its first quarter
and fiscal year 2026 outlook, as provided in connection with fourth quarter and fiscal year 2025 financial results on August 14, 2025\.
The Company continues to expect Adjusted EPS of 80\-83 cps, representing 12\-17% constant currency growth, and Free Cash Flow of $1\.8\-1\.9
billion for fiscal year 2026\. For the first quarter, Amcor also expects Adjusted EPS to be within the previously announced 18\-20 cps range\.
