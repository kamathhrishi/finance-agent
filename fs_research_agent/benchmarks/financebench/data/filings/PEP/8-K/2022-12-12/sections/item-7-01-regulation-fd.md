*Pursuant

by and between PepsiCo and J\.P\. Morgan Trust Company, National Association, as trustee, to whom the Trustee succeeded in interest\.*

## Certain United States Federal Income Tax Considerations
PepsiCo expects that the Defeasance will be treated as a taxable exchange
of the Notes for U\.S\. federal income tax purposes\. Holders of Notes are urged to consult their tax advisors as to the specific consequences
of the Defeasance to them\.
